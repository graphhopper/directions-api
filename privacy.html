Privacy

Last modified: Mar 19, 2018

The GraphHopper GmbH is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable user data, except what you voluntarily give to us via email, up on registration or other direct contact from you. We'll not sell or rent this information to anyone.

GraphHopper Website and Documentation

For web analytics we use Matomo (piwik) where we do not store the full IP address but only 2 bytes like 192.168.xxx.xxx, as well as information like your internet domain, the date and time of your visit, and the pages you access on GraphHopper.com. This information is collected solely for diagnostic and analytic purposes. We do not share any identifiable information about this information at any time, unless required by law, and use this information internally and only to better assess the services provided through GraphHopper.com.

If you contact us via our contact form or Email the data is stored in our CRM system hosted in Germany. If you contact us via Email the emails are stored at our Email-provider DomainFactory hosted in Germany.

GraphHopper Maps

GraphHopper Maps is part of the GraphHopper website and so the same policy applies. Additionally we use the default map provider Omniscale where the request is processed to serve the map tiles.

Cookies

We use cookies information to
(i) store information so you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or in subsequent visits;
(ii) maintain sessions for authenticated users; and
(iii) monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of visitors and pages viewed.

Cookies are not used for the GraphHopper Directions API.

GraphHopper Directions API

For the GraphHopper Directions API we collect personal information of the customer, such as name, email, registration IP, time and company name. The purpose is to send technical emails, support contact, privacy or term changes, newsletter (only if agreed), the obligation to prove the business nature of the registration request and to reduce likelihood of misusage of credit card information at a later stage. To avoid newsletter Emails or if you want to request deletion of your account you can unsubscribe via a link provided at the end of every newsletter or send us a message via the dashboard.

We process the API requests on rented servers from Hetzner hosted in Germany. As the geographic coordinates might be personal information we take special care.

Every API request is stored and we save the associated information (request body and headers, IP, time) for a maximum of 5 weeks. We use this to identify misusage of API tokens, improve the API usage from our customers and to improve performance and usability of our API. Our customers have the choice to use a proxy to enhance the privacy of their users.

Certain Geocoding requests with an external provider go to OpenCageData and Gisgraphy.

Customers can request their personal information as well as requesting the deletion of them via dashboard message or Email. Personal information like Email can be changed in the dashboard or requested via the dashboard.

Payment providers can store additional information such as your address, and credit card number. This information is used solely for billing purposes and to fill your orders. It is collected, processed, and held in a secure environment that only authorized persons can access, still credit card information is not stored only processed via our payment provider. We use the subscription provider billwerk (monthly recurring invoices), the payment provider Heidelpay and the payment networks Visa, Mastercard and American Express depending on the customers its credit card type.

Passwords are stored encrypted and we take precautions to protect your information. When you exchange information via our Services, your information is protected both, online via HTTPS, PFS and similar techniques, as well as offline due to modern and secure datacenters.

For invoice processing we use services from R/F/E and Buchhaltungsbutler and Billwerk uses Mailjet to send invoices.
